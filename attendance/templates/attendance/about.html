About AMU Attendance System

Our Mission

The AMU Attendance System is designed to streamline and modernize the attendance tracking process at Aligarh Muslim University. Our mission is to provide a reliable, efficient, and user-friendly platform for managing student attendance across all departments and courses.

Key Features

  • 📊 Real-time attendance tracking
  • 👨‍🏫 Faculty-friendly interface
  • 📱 Student self-service portal
  • 📈 Detailed attendance reports
  • 🔒 Secure authentication system

System Benefits

For Students
  • Easy access to attendance records
  • Real-time notifications
  • Transparent tracking system
For Faculty
  • Simplified attendance marking
  • Automated report generation
  • Efficient class management
